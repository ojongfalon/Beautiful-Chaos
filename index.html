Beautiful Chaos

You are a whirlwind wrapped in stardust,

A mystery stitched in moonlight.

You bloom where silence softly grows,

A presence that outshines the past.

You are beautiful chaos, unrefined,

The art of a storm in a delicate mind.

And even when you pull away,

I see the moon in your quiet sway.

You walk like poetry spilling over the margins

Of a world that's too loud, too bright, too fast.

Yet in your eyes, I find a calm,

A universe where I can breathe at last.

In the chaos of your laughter, I find my peace,

A symphony of whispers, a sweet release.

In chaos, love still finds its grace—

A home inside your quiet space.

— Always yours, Carrots
